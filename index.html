Bigfoot

Click the pictures to view the source.

Bigfoot are a species of apelike creatures that walk upright, like humans, but are far larger and more reclusive, leading to their scarce sightings.

Stats:

(Note, stats are numbered so that 10 = the human average.)

  • STRength: 20

    20 strength is incredibly high, this creature is not to be trifled with, long range will be your best bet if the creature picks a fight with you.

  • DEXterity: 8

    Many sightings of Bigfoot claim it to be a lumbering tower. Walking without much grace, or speed. Likely has diminished reflexes, however, try to avoid trees. As a descendent of apes, the Bigfoot likely has enhanced climbing prowess.

  • CHArisma: 8

    Out of sight, out of mind. Bigfoot, as far as we know, are not likely to make contact with other species, their nature among peers has yet to be studied.

  • WISdom: 16

    Spending the entirety of it's life in the woods, it has become accustomed to nature, and may have learned druidic spells.

  • INTellect: 10

    This creature may or may not know much, but considering it avoids us, I'd like to think it might be a little more intelligent than current knowledge tends to suggest.

  • Class: Grappler

The Loch Ness Monster

Click the pictures to view the source.

The Loch Ness Monster is a creature that resides in the great Loch Ness. It has a serpent like body structure, and resembles a plesiosaur. The monster has been spoken about for many centuries and only grainy photographs have ever been taken of it. Be warned, this creature also resembles some depictions of basilisks.

Stats:

(Note, stats are numbered so that 10 = the human average.)

  • STRength: 16

    The Loch Ness Monster is powerful, but not the most powerful creature around.

  • DEXterity: 18

    The Loch Ness Monster has a staggering dexterity of 18,it moves through the water with grace and speed.

  • CHArisma: ?

    There is no knowledge of this creatures sociability.

  • WISdom: 14

    This creature is wise enough to avoid pretty much everything.

  • INTellect: 8

    This creature's intellect is not apparent, but its elusiveness implies it has some.

  • Class: Neutral, Tricky

The Wendigo

This image is from: Pixabay

The Wendigo is a creature born from the hunger of cannibals. They hunt their prey relentlessly with razor sharp teeth, limbs that reach far distances, and lightning fast reflexes. Some depictions show the Wendigo as having limited perception, they can only see moving objects. If all else fails, this will be your fallback.

Stats:

(Note, stats are numbered so that 10 = the human average.)

  • STRength: 20

    Despite the Wendigo's gaunt appearance, they posess super human strength. Try to keep as much distance as possible, and do not run.

  • DEXterity: 20

    The Wendigo has long reaching, dexterous limbs that it uses to maneuver itself through the harsh northern terrain.

  • CHArisma: 0

    These creatures are hunger incarnate, and with all the negative feelings that hunger brings, I cant imagine this creature being sociable.

  • WISdom: 0

    These creatures are purely instinctual, they have no patience or composure.

  • INTellect: 14

    This creature hunts using methods that suggest intelligence, baiting and ambushing prey with it's ability to mimic voices.

  • Class: Rushdown

Chupacabra

The Chupacabra is a creature of legend from Mexico. The name "Chupacabra" literally translates to "Goat Sucker" it gets it's nutrients from the blood of unsuspecting livestock.

Stats:

(Note, stats are numbered so that 10 = the human average.)

  • STRength: 8

    The Chupacabra is quite weak in comparison to the other cryptids. It trades raw power for stealth and speed to ambush it's prey.

  • DEXterity: 20

    The Chupacabra is small enough and mobile enough to win most matchups, against considerably larger opponents, it tends to flounder.

  • CHArisma: 0

    This thing's personality is on par with that of a rabid dog.

  • WISdom: 0

    Rabid dog type personality.

  • INTellect: 0

    The Chupacabra has no capability of higher thought, it is an entirely instinctual animal.

  • Class: Rushdown.
